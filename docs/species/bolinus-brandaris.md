The purple dye murex (*Bolinus brandaris*) is a predatory muricid gastropod historically exploited in antiquity for Tyrian purple dye. It inhabits sandy and muddy substrates where it preys on bivalves and other molluscs. Still commercially collected for food in the Aegean.
