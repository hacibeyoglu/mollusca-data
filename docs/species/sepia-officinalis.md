The common cuttlefish (*Sepia officinalis*) is a highly adaptable cephalopod found throughout Turkish seas. It possesses remarkable colour-change abilities for camouflage and communication. The internal cuttlebone is distinctive and often washed ashore on beaches. Commercially fished in the Aegean and Levantine.
