# Marine Mollusca of Turkish Waters

This field guide covers **963 species** across five classes recorded in Turkish marine waters, drawn from GBIF occurrence data and cross-referenced with WoRMS-compatible taxonomy.

The catalogue spans **Bivalvia** (mussels, clams, oysters, scallops), **Cephalopoda** (octopuses, squids, cuttlefishes), **Gastropoda** (snails, limpets, sea slugs, whelks), **Polyplacophora** (chitons), and **Scaphopoda** (tusk shells).

Use the sidebar to browse by class, order, and family.

---

*Taxonomy follows WoRMS (World Register of Marine Species). Conservation status reflects known assessments; many species remain unevaluated.*
