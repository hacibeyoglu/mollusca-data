The Mediterranean mussel (*Mytilus galloprovincialis*) is one of the most commercially important bivalves in Turkish waters. Dense colonies form on hard substrates in the intertidal and shallow subtidal zones. It is widely harvested and farmed throughout the Marmara and Black Sea coasts.
