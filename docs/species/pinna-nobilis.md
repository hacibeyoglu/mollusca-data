The noble pen shell (*Pinna nobilis*) is the largest endemic bivalve in the Mediterranean Sea, reaching up to 120 cm. It anchors itself in soft sediments using a byssus and is considered Critically Endangered due to mass mortality events caused by the parasite *Haplosporidium pinnae*.
